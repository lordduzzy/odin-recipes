Mama's Lasagna

Passed down for generations, this recipe is designed for the most delicate palates. Everyone is so creative, but this thing will be the best you've eaten.

Below are the ingredents:

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Secret seasoning

To make the lasagna:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. layer sauce on bottom of pan
  3. Layer cheese
  4. layer noods
  5. repeat till the pan is full
